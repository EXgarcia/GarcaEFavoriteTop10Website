Elizar's Top 10 Favorite Playstation Games/Series


Welcome to my Top 10 page. Down below are my favorite games/series of all time that I played on Playstation.
I based these chart off of Nostalgia, Amount of hours put into the game, incentives, skill level, and in game customization.
Not all games hit each subject but they hit enough of one or more to make it on my Top 10 list.




#10. Star Wars Episode One Jedi Power Battles

Go somewhere
Review:
Star Wars Episode 1 Jedi Power Battles is hand down one of the most underrated games of the ps1 era. You start off with a handful of Jedi Masters that have all of their own special powers. My favorite character was Plo Koon. Some of his powers involved shooting an energy blast from his hand, using the force as a bubble to block incoming shots from blasters, and a force push that pushed everything away around him. Its not a very long game, there are only ten levels to the game and a few bonus levels that you have to unlock. The combo system is easy but extremely satisfying, taking Some of the moves right our of the movie. I beat this game into the ground as a kid! Unlocking everything I could, getting every combo I could! Till this day there has not been a Star Wars game that pulled me in like this one.




#9. Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2

Go somewhere
Review: The Call of Duty franchise is what got me into online competitive gaming. MW2 was my favorite of the series. The customization of weapons, titles and badges seemed endless at the time. The amount of work you had to put in to unlock these items were addicting. Jumping on with friends to take on other teams was a rush. Gettiing killstreaks was such a satisfying pay off on top of just owning the other team. I've spent many of moons honing my skills on the games to get the unlock and i know for a fact that I never completely unlocked everything. The amount of weapons on this game was amazing. I was an annoying camper and when that didnt work Noobtubing was the way. Noobtubing is actually why this is my favorite C.O.D, it was so annoying and OP but they never got rid of it. The amount of rage it would put in people made it all the better. the Fan Mail I would get from this game was never disappointing.
