Elizar's Top 10 Favorite Playstation Games/Series


Home Page

Welcome to my Top 10 page. Down below are my favorite games/series of all time that I played on Playstation.
I based these chart off of Nostalgia, Amount of hours put into the game, incentives, skill level, and in game customization. Not all games hit each subject but they hit enough of one or more to make it on my Top 10 list.




#10. Star Wars Episode One Jedi Power Battles

YouTube Review
Review:
Star Wars Episode 1 Jedi Power Battles is hands down one of the most underrated games of the ps1 era. You start off with a handful of Jedi Masters that have all of their own special powers. My favorite character was Plo Koon. Some of his powers involved shooting an energy blast from his hand, using the force as a bubble to block incoming shots from blasters, and a force push that pushed everything away around him. Its not a very long game, there are only ten levels to the game and a few bonus levels that you have to unlock. The combo system is easy but extremely satisfying, taking Some of the moves right out of the movie. I beat this game into the ground as a kid! Unlocking everything I could, getting every combo I could! Till this day there has not been a Star Wars game that pulled me in like this one.

Review:
The Call of Duty franchise is what got me into online competitive gaming. MW2 was my favorite of the series. The customization of weapons, titles and badges seemed endless at the time. The amount of work you had to put in to unlock these items were addicting. Jumping on with friends to take on other teams was a rush. Gettiing killstreaks was such a satisfying pay off on top of just owning the other team. I've spent many of moons honing my skills on the games to get the unlock and i know for a fact that I never completely unlocked everything. The amount of weapons on this game was amazing. I was an annoying camper and when that didnt work Noobtubing was the way. Noobtubing is actually why this is my favorite C.O.D, it was so annoying and OP but they never got rid of it. The amount of rage it would put in people made it all the better. the Fan Mail I would get from this game was never disappointing.




#8. Battlefield 4

YouTube Review
Review:
BattleField 4 was my way out of Call Of duty. It plays at a much slower pace. On top of that you can drive around in vehicles like tanks, jeeps, boats and fly in aircrafts. The maps are also huge! It really to sniping to another level. The bullet drop was a game changer that took time to adjust to. The classes were set up completely diffent but it worked out once you took the time to understand them. Ive spent man moons trying to rank up in this game and rarely ever became bored with the game. You also had full customization over your player badges which led to some unique designs. For the weapons it was simialr to COD as in the weapon add ons and skins. Being able to control so many vehicles and aircrafts is what really stuck out about this game. It took ours to get everyone of them Down making it very challenging but rewarding. Would definetly pick Battlefield over COD any day.




#7. Devil May Cry - Series

YouTube Review
Review:
The Devil May Cry series will always be amazing to me. Playing as a demon that kills other demons!!! I mean come on!! Fighting with Style is how you get the max score and most fun out of it. There are a variety of weapons that add to his stylish fighting. From a sword, to nunchucks, to gauntlets, you woulld have to cycle through these weapons to kill your enemy. Every game in the series is fun and addicting. The more the series continued the bigger and harder the enemy would become. In your fight to keep you hell from taking over you learn that our main character has family ties to the Devil himself. Going through your journey discovering hidden mini games and weapons to add to your arsenal, Playing rarely became boring. At a certain point in the game you gain your devil trigger ability, Which turns you into an over powered flying demon. The graphics to the cinematics were always so beautiful and progressive. It's a little hard to get the fighting style down in the beginning but learning how to mix up your weapons and gun along with your devil trigger you learn how to tear through the enemies and bosses.




#6. Guitar Hero - Series

YouTube Review
Review:
I miss Guitar Hero so much!!! I wish I could be playing this game today! The more this series grew the more diverse their songs became. I remember spending hour trying to get the speed and technique down so I could unlocks songs and move onto higher difficulties. I eventually worked up to expert+ but geez it took a lot of time. Eventually they added a drumset and a mic, finally completing the band. That drumset was so fun and brought a diffetent perspective to the game. Guitar Hero would be the highlight of some hamg outs and it ever failed. There was even ways to take some songs out and add any song you would like. I miss this game so much, I miss blasting my tv full volume playing the same songs over and over so i can get down to almost perfection. They added co-op modes and competitive modes with online playing which was decent but not the best. If you're a music lover but do not quite have what it takes to be a musician, this game is a good middle to that, even on easy mode you can feel like a rock star.




#5. Bloody Roar - Series

YouTube Review
Review:
Bloody Roar, I think, will alwasy be my favorite fighter game. Having a fighter game where your character can change into a beast just takes the cake. There was nothing else like it, as the series went on they added specials to the charaacters which made the game even better. The combos were beautiful and if you knew how you could chain them into your beast mode which could lead into your specials. Tiger biting heads, wolves biting thoats, and an overgrow mole stabbing through people was intense!! Sadly there really isn't much else to say about the game. It had an arcade style to it, so there really wasn't a story mode to it. You would get a cinematic for each character after beating the arcade mode. They had cheats in the game that would make the walls disappear or the hand and or heads really big. After an hour, unless you were playing with friends, it would feel kind of repetitive after awhile. This one was highly nostalgia driven but I recommend watching the video that is provided.




#4. Metal Gear - Series

YouTube Review
Review:
Metal Gear is game that still continues to grow through the consoles. The last addition to the series was not the best but what do you expect after losing Hideo Kojima. This game is all about Tactical Espionage, stealth kills was key!! If you didn't have a silencer then you could knock them out with punches or a sleeper hold. The variety of guns that the game had to offer grew with each game. They would add thing to each game so it would get you to play it over and ever again. Adding easter eggs everywhere. Some of the challenges were collection dog tags from almost every character or boss in the game. Changing difficulties would change the amount of soldiers so if you couldnt get the hardest modes you werent going to able to get all of the dog tags, thats for Metal Gear Solid 2. In MGS3 they had a similar approach but you would take bosses clothes that came with a special ability. It continues like this and expands to more challenges making repeating this game so many times not a problem. The cinematics were always filled with drama and action, some were a little to long tho. In one of the games a cutscene took an hour to complete. I wasn't to fond of it but it was actually very important. If you into being a stealth assassin them you would enjoy this game. You can also go loud with your guns but you were ALWAYS outnummbered so it mad it a lot harder to try and progress, in no way is this like COD.




#3. The Last of Us

YouTube Review
Review:
The Last of Us is another stealth driven game. Stealth being key to survival, not just to survive humanity attacks but also the infected(zombies). The Story to this game was so good that they're making an HBO series to it. You feel the emotions of these characters from what they deal with. This was the first game I platinumed on PSN, I played it over and over and over on every difficulty. I'm still not tired of the storyline, this game really just kicks you in the feels from beginning to end. The online play for it is still extremely good for how old this game is. Its slow paced and tactical, you start off with a lot less ammo then most games. With that as a factor you really are forced to think about the decisions you make. If you are shooting at everything and hitting nothing then you will die without a doubt. Taking it slow and being smart about your ammo and weapons is the key to being successful. There are so many jump scares on this game because you become so focused on trying to be stealthy. Its always fun watching and or hearing someone get scared on this game. This game is a must play, if not for online play just play the story mode, it is worth it!!!!




#2. Rocket League

YouTube Review
Review:
Rocket League is so hard!!! The mechanics on this game is insane!! Using a car to play soccer sounds kind of weird but it's so fun!!! The cars can fly as well!! Adding to the difficulty of this game. It is a rough start in the beginning but with practice you kind of get a feel for what should and should not be done. The customization to this game is insane!! So many things to choose from cars, skins, rims, boosts, trails, goals, toppers, antennas, music theme, and engine sounds. Theres just so much to choose from, so much!!! Hundreds and hundreds of items to choose from to put on your car. They usually have themed events for like christman and halloween, tournaments, giveaways, and also for the cars they will bring in things from movies, tv shows and other games. You can get different versioons of the BatMobile, the Dolorian, cars from fast and the furious, sweet tooths ice cream truck from twisted metal, and so much more. there just so much customization to this game to make your car really feel like its just your car. I would play this game every day to get the daily challenges and season challenges done. The skill level for this game is so high it's ridiculous. This game does not disappoint but it can be very frustrating. The amount of work they are constantly putting in the game has earned its spot as number 2 in the list.
